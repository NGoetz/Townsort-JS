Townsort-Device

Two cities are given for example. Please enter your data in the given structure. You may edit the data whenever you want.

You have to give the coordinates in one of the both ways shown (you can put the hemisphere information wherever you want). The programm will convert the coordinates into decimal degrees. If you don't know the minutes or seconds, this is not a problem. Negative values indicate the southern/western hemisphere. You can also get your current position via Google.

  2. Examplecity2; 54°5'20''N; 12°8'O; 310000
